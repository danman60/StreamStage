# Talk 1 Slides — VIDEO
**The Content Day: A Year of Video in One Morning**
Daniel Abrahamson · StreamStage · Dance NYC Expo
Built off `talk1-video-script.md`. 13 slides. ~20 min + Q&A.

Design notes (apply to all slides):
- Big-stage-readable: max ~7 words/line, ≤3 lines of body text. Headline-driven, not paragraph-driven.
- Fonts: bold sans headers, clean body. High contrast for a lit trade-show hall.
- Slide text is a PROMPT for Daniel, never a teleprompter — he speaks the room, the slide anchors.
- Markers mirror the script: **[INTERACT] [LIVE EXAMPLE] [DEMO]**.
- Guardrails: no exact price on any slide, no client names without **[confirm]**. Booking QR = streamstage.live/book.
- Sibling-talk rule: NO printer joke, NO livestream-email story, NO StudioSage product content — those belong to Talk 2. Only the Slide 12 cliffhanger references Talk 2.
