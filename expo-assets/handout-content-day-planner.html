The four stations

Interview / story

The heart. Owners, teachers, dancers on camera — the story only your studio can tell.

The brand reel. Same lights, same look — a consistent feed all year.

Extra angles

Every device is a focal length. Drone, GoPro, phone — coverage stacks up.

What to prep before the day

  • Pick the date everyone can make — hair, makeup, costumes ready (treat it like picture day, but for video)
  • List your 3 hero routines + 3 people with a story worth telling
  • Gather brand bits: logo file, studio colors, the phrases you always say
  • Warn the building about the hazer (ask us — it's worth it)
  • Block one morning. That's the whole ask.

What you walk out with

  • Homepage story video — the one that makes new parents pick you
  • Living background video — your website stops looking like a brochure
  • Recital + competition reels — consistent brand, every post
  • Dozens of social cuts, scheduled — one clip becomes nine posts

Pro feeds UGC: keep posting your phone clips — the pro footage is the well you draw from all year. A 30-second edit can eat 2–4 hours of your night. We hand you the year.
