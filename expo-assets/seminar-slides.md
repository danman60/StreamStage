Design notes (apply to all slides):
- Big-stage-readable: max ~7 words/line, ≤3 lines of body text. Headline-driven, not paragraph-driven.
- Fonts: bold sans headers, clean body. High contrast for a lit trade-show hall.
- Slide text is a PROMPT for Daniel, never a teleprompter — he speaks the room, the slide anchors.
- Markers mirror the script: **[INTERACT] [LIVE EXAMPLE] [DEMO] [LIVE ANIMATED DEMO]**.
- Guardrails: no exact price on any slide, no beta-client names, no saved-hours stat unless Daniel clears it live. URL on screen = **studiosage.ai**. Booking QR = streamstage.live/book.
