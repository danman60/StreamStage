# Talk 2 Slides — AI
**The AI Front Desk: Stop Answering the Same Question Forever**
Daniel Abrahamson · StreamStage · Dance NYC Expo
Built off `talk2-ai-script.md`. 17 slides. ~20 min + Q&A.

Design notes (apply to all slides):
- Big-stage-readable: max ~7 words/line, ≤3 lines of body text. Headline-driven, not paragraph-driven.
- Fonts: bold sans headers, clean body. High contrast for a lit trade-show hall.
- Slide text is a PROMPT for Daniel, never a teleprompter — he speaks the room, the slide anchors.
- Markers mirror the script: **[INTERACT] [LIVE EXAMPLE] [DEMO] [LIVE ANIMATED DEMO]**.
- Guardrails: no exact price on any slide, no beta-client names, no saved-hours stat unless Daniel clears it live. URL on screen = **studiosage.ai**.
- Sibling-talk rule: NO content-day material, NO drone/mortgage/hostage jokes — those belong to Talk 1. Only Slide 17's close may wink at Talk 1.
- The five TIP slides share a visual template: big "FREE MOVE #N" tag top-left, one-line pain, one-line move, boxed "MONDAY:" action at the bottom. The repetition of the template teaches the room the rhythm.
